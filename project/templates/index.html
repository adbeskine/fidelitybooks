Welcome to Fidelity Books

Fidelity Books specialises in knowledge, experience and expertise. Fidelity publishes valuable, interesting or worthwhile knowledge and expertise gained by people from their own experience. Our particular focus is on the lives of individual people, families, work and the wider community.

We are primarily interested in real people and their day-to-day lives. We are interested in what people have learned, undertaken or experienced as part of their life. We are interested in the wisdom people have gained or the knowledge they have developed.

We are interested, in the broadest sense, in the knowledge and expertise that people have accumulated in the course of their life, the dissemination of which might be to the benefit of others.

Our initial publications include books on the following five topics:

    • How to bring up children so they never smoke or take drugs
    • The secret rules for surviving life in the office and at work
    • How to find good builders
    • A jokes book
    • A guide to granite kitchen worktops

These books are based upon experience built up over a lifetime. All are relevant to other individuals, families, work or the wider community.

Our publications can be seen on our BOOKSHOP Page  By clicking on an individual book, you will see further information on its content and how to buy it.

Fidelity Books is pleased to hear from authors who want to submit manuscripts for publication based upon their knowledge, experience or expertise. We particularly encourage unpublished authors who feel that they have a valuable, interesting or unique story to tell.

New authors can submit their proposals via our Authors' Page
